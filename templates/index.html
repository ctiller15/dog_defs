What is this?

DogDefs was made with a simple goal: to gather all of the crazy words in the dog world into one place! This is to serve as a free resource for all dog enthusiasts both new and experienced.

And yes, the plan is to have it be free, with no ads, forever

With that out of the way, why don't you explore a little bit? You can check out our list of words, search for something (look up), or add your own!

Thanks for visiting, hope you find it useful!
